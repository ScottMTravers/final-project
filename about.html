Mark Johnson | PGA Professional

Mark Johnson started MJ Golf Instruction in the winter of 2014. Mark has been the Head Professional at Riverside Golf Course in Fresno, CA, from 2004-present. In 1992, he earned his PGA Tour card through the Qualifying School. After helping other professionals and wanting to spend more time with his family, Mark found that teaching was his true calling. He has Tiger Woods, among other professionals reach the pinaccle of the sport.

Brad Stephenson | Assistant Professional

Brad Stephenson has been the right hand man for Mark since the school opened in 2014. Brad has been the Assistant Professional at Riverside Golf Course since 2008. He specializes in the physics of the golf swing, and prides his knowledge and work with launch monitors.

David Small | Assistant Professional

David Small joined MJ Golf Instruction in the fall of 2014. He has been at the forefront of junior golf development in the Central Valley. Emphasizing in fundamentals and controlling the mind, David is notorious for getting players out of slumps. Ask for David if something in your game isn't quite right!
